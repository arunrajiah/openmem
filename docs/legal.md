# Legal stance

OpenMem captures conversations that the authenticated user is already viewing
in their own browser session. Specifically:

- We do **not** circumvent authentication.
- We do **not** access other users' data.
- We do **not** transmit captured data off the user's device.
- We do **not** scrape provider sites headlessly or at scale.

The model is equivalent to the user copy-pasting their own transcripts into a
local notebook — automated for convenience.

Each provider's Terms of Service may have clauses governing automated access
or data extraction. Users are responsible for their own compliance. Where a
provider offers an official data export (OpenAI export, Anthropic export,
Google Takeout), that is always the preferred source.
